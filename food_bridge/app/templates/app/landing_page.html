Welcome to Food Bridge

Food Bridge is a tech-powered initiative that connects surplus food from donors to those who need it most.

With over 189.2 million undernourished people in India alone, we knew ending hunger completely would be a monumental task. But we chose to take a small, impactful step—bridging the gap between food excess and food scarcity.

Food Bridge started in city pune across India. Our mission is to ensure that perfectly edible food never goes to waste and instead reaches hungry mouths in time.

From weddings and corporate events to restaurants and home gatherings—food often remains unused and ends up being discarded. Food Bridge helps you make a better choice: donate that food with just a few clicks and let it nourish someone in need.

Join us today in fighting hunger and food waste. Your small act of kindness can mean the world to someone else.

How It Works

For Donors

Farms, restaurants, cafeterias, hotels, stadiums, and grocery stores post excess food in under a minute on the Waste No Food app.

For NGOs

Verified charities are instantly notified of food donations and can claim those they can use to feed people in need..

Our Impact

Once the food is claimed, a charity or group of volunteers picks it up and shares it with hungry individuals..
